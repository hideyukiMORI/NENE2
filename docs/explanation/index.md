# Explanation

Explanation articles discuss the *why* behind NENE2's design decisions. They are not step-by-step guides — they are background reading that helps you understand the reasoning and trade-offs.

## Articles

- [Why PSR Standards?](why-psr) — why PSR-7, PSR-15, and PSR-17 instead of custom HTTP abstractions
- [Why Explicit Dependency Wiring?](why-explicit-wiring) — why hand-written DI instead of autowiring
- [Why RFC 9457 Problem Details?](why-problem-details) — why a standard error format instead of a custom shape
- [Why MCP as the AI Integration Boundary?](why-mcp) — why AI agents go through the API, not the database
